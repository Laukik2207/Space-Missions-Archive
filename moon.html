MOON

Humanity’s first destination beyond Earth. A symbol of ambition, exploration, and the beginning of space travel.

Historic Moon Missions

Apollo 11 (1969)

First successful human landing on the Moon. Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin made history.

Apollo 17 (1972)

The last crewed mission to the Moon during the Apollo program.

Chandrayaan-3 (2023)

India’s historic landing near the Moon’s south pole.

Artemis I (2022)

NASA’s return-to-Moon program preparing for future human exploration.

Moon Exploration Timeline

1959

Luna 2 becomes the first human-made object to reach the Moon.

1966

Luna 9 achieves the first successful soft landing on the Moon.

1968

Apollo 8 becomes the first crewed mission to orbit the Moon.

1969

Apollo 11 achieves the first human Moon landing.

1972

Apollo 17 becomes the final crewed Moon landing mission.

1994

Clementine mission maps the lunar surface in detail.

2008

Chandrayaan-1 confirms the presence of water molecules.

2013

China’s Chang’e 3 lands successfully on the Moon.

2019

Chang’e 4 achieves the first landing on the far side.

2022

Artemis I completes an uncrewed mission around the Moon.

2023

Chandrayaan-3 lands near the Moon’s south pole.
