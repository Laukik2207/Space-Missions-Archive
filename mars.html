MARS

The Red Planet — humanity’s next frontier. A world of dust storms, ancient water, and future colonies.

Historic Mars Missions

Viking 1 (1976)

First successful Mars landing and surface experiment.

Pathfinder (1997)

Introduced the first Mars rover, Sojourner.

Curiosity (2012)

Discovered evidence of ancient habitable environments.

Perseverance (2021)

Searching for signs of ancient microbial life.

Mars Exploration Timeline

1965

Mariner 4 performs the first successful Mars flyby.

1976

Viking 1 becomes the first spacecraft to land successfully.

1997

Mars Pathfinder lands and deploys the Sojourner rover.

2012

Curiosity rover begins exploring Gale Crater.

2021

Perseverance lands in Jezero Crater with Ingenuity helicopter.

2030s

Planned human missions aim to reach Mars.
