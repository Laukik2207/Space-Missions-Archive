Mars Exploration Timeline

1965

Mariner 4 performs the first successful flyby of Mars, returning the first close-up images of the planet.

1976

Viking 1 becomes the first spacecraft to successfully land on Mars and conduct surface experiments.

1997

Mars Pathfinder lands and deploys the Sojourner rover, proving mobile exploration on Mars is possible.

2012

Curiosity Rover lands using the revolutionary sky crane system to study Mars’ habitability.

2021

Perseverance Rover lands in Jezero Crater and begins searching for signs of ancient life.

Future

Human missions and potential Mars colonies are being actively planned for the coming decades.
